# Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior and shopping trends using a retail dataset. The goal is to extract actionable insights related to customer segmentation, purchasing patterns, and revenue drivers.

An interactive dashboard was built to visualize key metrics such as revenue distribution, customer segmentation, subscription impact, and demographic trends.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns across product categories
- Identify high-revenue and high-demand segments
- Understand the impact of subscription status on customer behavior
- Explore demographic trends (age, gender)
- Provide business recommendations to improve revenue and engagement

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Data Storage & Querying)
- Power BI (Dashboard Visualization)
- Excel (Initial Exploration)


## 📂 Dataset
- Retail customer shopping behavior dataset
- Includes: Category, Purchase Amount, Age, Gender, Subscription Status, Shipping Type, Review Rating


## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue Insights
- Clothing category generates the highest revenue (~100K+), contributing significantly to total sales
- Accessories follow as the second-highest revenue driver
- Outerwear contributes the least revenue, indicating low demand or pricing issues

### 🛍️ Customer Behavior
- Total customers: ~3.9K
- Average purchase amount: $59.76
- Average review rating: 3.75

### 📦 Subscription Impact
- 73% of customers are non-subscribers (~2.85K)
- Only 27% are subscribed (~1.05K)
➡️ Indicates huge opportunity for subscription growth

### 👥 Demographic Insights
- Young adults generate the highest revenue (~60K+)
- Middle-aged customers also contribute significantly
- Seniors and adults show slightly lower engagement

### 📊 Sales Trends
- Clothing has the highest number of purchases (~1700+)
- Outerwear has the lowest sales (~300–400)


## 📌 Business Recommendations

- **Increase Subscription Adoption**
  - Offer discounts or loyalty rewards to convert the 73% non-subscribers
  - Introduce exclusive benefits for members

- **Focus on High-Performing Categories**
  - Expand inventory and marketing for Clothing & Accessories

- **Improve Low-Performing Categories**
  - Re-evaluate pricing or marketing strategy for Outerwear

- **Target Young Adults**
  - Run personalized campaigns as they contribute the most revenue

- **Enhance Customer Experience**
  - Improve product quality to increase average rating (currently 3.75)
